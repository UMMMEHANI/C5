SKATING

Skating is a sport that involves gliding on ice or other surfaces using shoes or boots with wheels or bladelike runners attached to the bottom. It can be a fun hobby or a competitive sport, and there are many different types of skating to choose from: Ice skating Can be done for fun indoors or outdoors, and includes competitive sports like figure skating and speed skating. Speed skating is a competitive race where skaters race each other over a set distance. Roller skating Involves skating on circles inscribed on the skating surface, and can include figures, which are prescribed movements made up of at least two circles. Ice hockey A team sport that involves skating and is played in schools, professional leagues, and at the Olympics. Tour skating A recreational form of long distance ice skating on natural ice. Skating is a great workout that engages many of the body's muscle groups and helps strengthen them.
